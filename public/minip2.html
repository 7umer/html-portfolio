Umer Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • Â¾ cup warm milk
  • 2¼ teaspoon yeast
  • ¼cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • cup blutter
  • 3 cups bread flour

for the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ Tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼cup butter

    • Instructions

    1. Mix the milk with the yeast.
    2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
    3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough.
    4. knead the gough for 10 minutes.
    5. Transfer th edough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
    6. After the dough has doubled in size roll it out into a large rectangle.
    7. preheat the oven to 3500F or 1800C, then bake the rolls for 20-25 min until lightly brown.
